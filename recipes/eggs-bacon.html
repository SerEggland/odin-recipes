Eggs and Bacon

Recipe Description

Sometimes the classic eggs and bacon is all you need.

This is a very simple recipe for eggs and bacon that anyone can make.

Ingredients

  • 2 extra large eggs
  • Your favorite bacon
  • A skillet

Instructions

  1. Fry your bacon in a skillet.
  2. Reduce heat as bacon starts to sizzle.
  3. Once bacon is cooked turn heat to medium low.
  4. Don't drain the bacon fat.
  5. Cook the eggs in the bacon fat.
  6. Place eggs and bacon on a plate and enjoy.
