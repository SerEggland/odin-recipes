Steak and Eggs

Recipe Description

There's nothing better than some good old Steak and Eggs in the morning.

Steak and Eggs is a hearty breakfast that will keep you going for hours on end. Plus being a big fan of eggs, there's nothing I'd rather have.

This is going to be a quick and easy recipe to kickstart your day the right way.

Ingredients

  • 2 (5 ounce) filet mignon
  • 1 tablespoon steak seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons clarified butter, divided
  • 2 large eggs

Instruction for Recipe

  1. Add seasoning to steak.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Melt butter in iron skillet over medium heat. Add steak and sear for 1 minute per side.
  4. Transfer skillet to oven and bake for 6 to 8 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and transfer to plate.
  6. Wipe excess grease from skillet, add butter. Break eggs and add into skillet.
  7. Cook eggs without letting them burn, 1 to 2 minutes.
  8. Remove from skillet and serve with steaks.
