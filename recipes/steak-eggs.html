Steak and Eggs

Recipe Description

There's nothing better than some good old Steak and Eggs in the morning.

Steak and Eggs is a hearty breakfast that will keep you going for hours on end. Plus being a big fan of eggs, there's nothing I'd rather have.

This is going to be a quick and easy recipe to kickstart your day the right way.

Ingredients

  • 2 (5 ounce) filet mignon
  • 1 tablespoon steak seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons clarified butter, divided
  • 2 large eggs
