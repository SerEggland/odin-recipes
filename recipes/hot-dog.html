Regular Hot Dog

Ingredients

  • 1 Hot Dog
  • Water (optional)
  • Martin's Long Potato Roll
  • Condiment of Choice
  • Topping of Choice

Instructions for Recipe

  1. Cook hot over medium heat on grill.
  2. Once hot dog is fully heated, remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Place hot dog in bun.
  4. Top with desired toppings and condiments.
